Micro Publication- typify

As part of a mini module, directed by Chris Murphy, we were tasked with creating an application or object that we can see being used in the future.

At the beginning of the first day we were spilt into teams and started the process of ideas generation on paper first.

The ideas from each team where shared with the class and each idea could be used as a base to start an individual project.
I really liked the idea of choosing a poster designed by a famous designer and having that delivered to you through the mail. So i took this idea and tried to solidify it.

typify

An app where monthly where a different designer movie genre is voted for by the app users. the highest voted designer for that month will then design a range of posters pins and sticker decals related to the film genre. The app users could choose from which deal they would like. From basic to deluxe.

The basic would include just the poster delivered to you and information about the movie and where to watch it.
The deluxe would include a signed poster with the pins and decals as well the information on the movie.
